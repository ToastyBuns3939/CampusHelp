In regional tour events, multiple stages will appear, each with different judging criteria and stage information.
On each stage, the assigned idols will perform. You can earn scores through these performances, and based on the scores obtained, you will earn event Pt.
Performance progress follows the same rules as tests during Produce, but all actions are performed automatically.
※For details, please see the Help sections “Lesson/Test” > “Test Flow” and “Contest.”

■ About Stage Clear
Each stage has a required score to clear it. Score progress is shared within the Circle, and when the total score reaches the required amount, the stage is considered cleared and you will receive stage clear rewards.
When all stages are cleared, the score progress for each stage will reset, and the round will advance. As the rounds progress, the clear score requirements and stage details will change.
※If you earn a score that exceeds the required amount while attempting a stage, the excess score will also count toward event Pt.

■ About Attempt Limits
There is a limit to how many times you can attempt a stage per day. Once you reach the limit, you can no longer attempt stages that day. The attempt count resets with the daily update.
