Jewels can be used to unlock any Mission Pass Points in Gasha, Packs, or to shorten job times, or in the Mission Pass.

There are paid jewels that can be purchased in the "Shop" and free jewels that can be obtained as rewards.

You can check the breakdown of jewels you own as follows.

・Long tap the jewel display in the header
・Display at the top of the "Shop" screen
・Display [Menu > Other > Check app-specific currency balance]

■ Consuming jewels
If you have paid and free jewels, the free jewels will be consumed first.

■ Linking paid jewels
Paid jewels cannot be linked to different platforms. They can only be used on the platform they were purchased on.
