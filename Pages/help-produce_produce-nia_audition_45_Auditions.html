In Auditions, you compete for rank against rivals, aiming to pass by placing 1st. Turns are randomly assigned as Vocal, Dance, or Visual, and your score increase rate depends on your Produce idol’s parameters, the evaluation criteria, bond effects, and total fan votes earned.

Before starting, you can choose which audition to enter—each audition has different evaluation criteria, rival strength, and reward amounts.
Some auditions require a certain number of fan votes to be eligible for entry.
If your score reaches the maximum possible during an audition, it will automatically end in success.

■ Audition Bonuses
Passing an audition grants you parameters and fan votes:

・Parameters
Increase based on the score earned in each corresponding turn (Vocal, Dance, Visual).
※The amount gained varies depending on the evaluation criteria.

・Fan Votes
Increase based on your total score during the audition.
