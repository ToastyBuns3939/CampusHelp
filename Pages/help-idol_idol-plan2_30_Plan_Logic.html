The plan indicates the direction of production and is set individually for each produce idol. The skill cards and types of P-Drinks that appear vary depending on the plan.

■Logic
A plan that focuses on growth by utilizing "Good Impression" and "Motivation."

・Good Impression
At the end of the turn, parameters increase by 1 for each point of Good Impression.
At the start of the turn, Good Impression decreases by 1.

・Motivation
For each 1 Motivation gained, Energy gain increases by 1.
