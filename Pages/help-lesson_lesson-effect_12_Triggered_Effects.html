Status enhancements or reductions caused by skill cards, P items, cheering, and so on will have various effects as long as they remain active on the field.

※If a new enhancement or reduction that wasn’t present at the start of the turn appears afterward, that status change will not decrease once due to turn progression.
※Effects triggered by changes in status only activate if the condition is met through the use of skill cards or P drinks. However, status changes caused by persistent effects like "Power of Wishes" or "Select Hatsuboshi Macchiato" will not trigger these effects.
