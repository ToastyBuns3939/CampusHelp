■Lesson Goals
Displays how much more the parameters need to increase to clear the lesson.
Once the goal is reached, it changes to the value for a Perfect Lesson.

■Remaining Turns
Displays the number of turns left until the lesson ends.

■Parameters
Shows the current values of the parameters being trained.

■Stamina & Energy
Displays the stamina and energy values during the lesson.
・Stamina: The value consumed when using skill cards.
・Energy: The value consumed in place of stamina during lessons. It is consumed before stamina.

■Skill Cards
Displays skill cards. For detailed explanations, see "Skill Cards."

■Cheer/Trouble
Shows the effects occurring during lessons and their activation conditions. Tap to see all ongoing effects.

■Active Effects
Tap icons to check the currently active buffs/debuffs.

■P-Items
Tap icons to check P items.

■P-Drinks
Tap icons to check and use P drinks.

■Possession Check
Check skill cards and P items you own. Also allows confirmation of the status of skill cards during lessons.
