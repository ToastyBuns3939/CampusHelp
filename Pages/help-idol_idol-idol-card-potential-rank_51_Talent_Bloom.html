You can use Produce Idol pieces to increase the talent bloom stage. As the talent bloom stage increases, parameter bonuses and maximum stamina will increase.

In addition, when the talent bloom stage reaches a certain level, unique P-items will be strengthened.

When the talent bloom stage increases further, P-Idol abilities will be unlocked.

When the talent bloom stage reaches its maximum, you can obtain a different color of the produce idol's costume.

The talent bloom stage can go up to four stages.

Produce Idol pieces can be obtained when you acquire a produce idol you already own.
