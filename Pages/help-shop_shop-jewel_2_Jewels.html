You can purchase Jewels, which are used for "Gacha" and pack purchases.

■ Date of Birth Registration
You must register your birthdate before your first Jewel purchase.
※Once registered, the date of birth cannot be changed.

■ Jewel Purchase Limits
・Under 16: Up to ¥5,000/month
・Age 16–17: Up to ¥20,000/month
・Age 18 and older: No limit

※For the DMM GAMES version, the currency is in points.

■ Jewel Holding Limit
You can hold up to 999,999,999 paid app-exclusive Jewels.
