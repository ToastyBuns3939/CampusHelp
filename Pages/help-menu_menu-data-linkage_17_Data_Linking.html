This feature links your game account with your Bandai Namco ID. By linking data, you can share game data across multiple devices and play on different devices. It also allows you to continue playing your game data if you switch to a new device or lose your device.

※Paid Jewels cannot be linked across different platforms and can only be used on the platform where they were purchased.
※Free Jewels can be linked across different platforms.
※Effects of the "Mission Pass" can also be linked across platforms, including achievement and acquisition status.
※Some saved data, such as Produce progress, cannot be transferred to different devices.
