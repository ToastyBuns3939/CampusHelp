Star Sparkles' are event items that can be obtained by completing productions and event missions during the event period, and can be used to draw event gacha.

You can earn more "Starlight" by producing in event mode than by normal producing during the event period.

You can also earn "Starlight" from lessons/self-study lessons and exams/auditions in event mode, as well as through the effects of event P items.

You can receive "Starlight" earned during producing by completing a production.

※You can also earn "Starlight" by completing normal producing during the event period.
※If you quit producing halfway through, you will not receive the "Starlight" earned during that producing.
※You cannot earn "Starlight" from lessons/self-study lessons and exams/auditions in normal producing.
