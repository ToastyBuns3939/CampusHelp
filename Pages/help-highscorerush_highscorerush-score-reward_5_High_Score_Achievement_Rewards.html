You can earn rewards by producing eligible idols in event mode during the event period and reaching a specified production rating.

■Idol-specific high score achievement reward
・This is a reward that can be earned by producing eligible idols in event mode during the event period and reaching a production rating above a specified value.
・One reward is set for each idol.

■Total high score achievement reward
・This is a reward that can be earned each time the total production rating recorded in the idol high score ranking for all eligible idols exceeds a certain value.
