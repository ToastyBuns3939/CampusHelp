In the Idol Commu section, you can view commus related to the idols.

■ Bond Commu
These are commus unlocked by increasing the idol's Bond Level.

■ Produce Commu
These are commus related to the idol that you can view during production. Some are unlocked by increasing the idol’s Affection Level.
Commus that are unlocked based on your production performance will also unlock lower-rank commus below your best result for that idol.

■ Produce Idol Commu
These are commus related to the produce idol, viewable during production. Some require higher Bond Level to unlock.

■ Other Commu
These are commus related to idols that can be viewed outside of “Produce.”
