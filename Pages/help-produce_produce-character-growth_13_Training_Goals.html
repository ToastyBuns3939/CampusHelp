You can check the current progress of an idol's training goals, Affection level, and True End achievements during Produce.

■Training Goals
These are the training tasks for the idol during Produce. Completing them will trigger a commu and grant rewards.

■Bond
You can view the current Bond level, its effects, and the conditions required to raise it. For more details on Bond, see the Help section under “Idols” → “Bond.”

■True End Achievements
Check the progress of achievements required to reach the True End. For more details on True End, see the Help section under “Produce” → “True End.”
