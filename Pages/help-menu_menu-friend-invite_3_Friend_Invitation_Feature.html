When a producer who has started the game enters an invite code, they become an "Invited Friend." Invited friends are different from regular friends. You can invite up to 100 people.

■ Invitation Target
・Producers who have cleared the Hatsuboshi Task 1

※Invited friends will appear in the list once they reach Producer Level 5.

■ Friend Invitation Rewards
Invitation missions are completed based on the invited friend's producer level, allowing you to earn Invitation Points. Rewards can be earned according to the total accumulated Invitation Points.
