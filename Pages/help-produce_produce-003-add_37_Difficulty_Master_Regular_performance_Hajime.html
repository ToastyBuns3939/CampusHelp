■Difficulty: Master (Regular Performance "Hajime")
A higher difficulty level of Produce compared to Regular and Pro.
Vocal, Dance, and Visual stat limits are increased, and with the effect of Challenge P-Items, the PERFECT cap for Lessons and SP Lessons also increases.

Only idols who have reached Bond Level 10 can attempt this.
"Rest" becomes selectable after performing one Lesson.

■Challenge P-Items
Can be selected during "Select Produce Idol" before starting Produce.
When starting Produce with a Challenge P-Item selected, various effects specific to the item will activate,
and the PERFECT cap for Lessons and SP Lessons will increase.
You can unlock up to 3 Challenge P-Items by meeting certain conditions.
Unlock conditions for the number of item slots must be fulfilled per idol.

※The available Challenge P-Items differ by Produce Idol.
※Unlocked item slots for Challenge P-Items are tracked separately for each idol.
