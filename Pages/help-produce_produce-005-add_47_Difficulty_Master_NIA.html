■Difficulty: Master (NEXT IDOL AUDITION)
A higher difficulty level of Produce compared to Pro.

Vocal, Dance, and Visual stat limits are increased, and with the effect of Challenge P-Items, the parameters gained from Audition Bonuses will also increase.

Only idols who have reached Bond Level 20 can attempt this.

■Challenge P-Items
Can be selected during "Select Produce Idol" before starting Produce.

When starting Produce with a Challenge P Item selected, various effects specific to the item will activate, and the parameters gained from Audition Bonuses will increase.

※The available Challenge P-Items differ by Produce Idol.
