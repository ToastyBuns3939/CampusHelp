■ N.I.A Master Ranking
・This is a ranking of Produce Evaluations earned on NEXT IDOL AUDITION at Master difficulty during the period.
・The ranking is separated by idol, and the highest Produce Evaluation score earned for each Produce Idol is reflected in that idol’s ranking.

※If scores are tied, the producer who reached that rank first will be placed higher.
※Produces started outside the N.I.A Master Ranking period will not be included in the ranking.

■ Ranking Rewards
・At the end of the aggregation period, the final rankings are determined, and rewards are granted according to each idol’s rank.
・Idols that do not complete a produce even once during the ranking period will be marked as non-participants and will not receive rewards.
