■About Unit Idols
These are produce idols whose lives and live shoots feature special performances where specific other idols also appear.

・About Units
A unit is made up of multiple idols.
In lives and live shoots during the production of a unit idol, other idols from the same unit will appear in the performance.

・About Unit Members
Produce idols who share the same song are considered unit members.
You can check unit members from idol details and unit confirmation screens.

■About Live Costumes
When starting a production or live shoot with a unit idol, you can also change the live costumes of other idols from the same unit (excluding the one you're producing).
If you own the produce idols of the same unit, those idols will appear in their own default costumes and hairstyles during the performance.

※If you do not own the produce idol for a unit member, they will appear in their default "Hajime" outfit and hairstyle during performances.

■About Focus Camera
When the Focus camera is ON, certain live scenes will focus on the produce idol using a special camera.
When the Focus camera is OFF, those scenes will be shown using a shared unit camera.
