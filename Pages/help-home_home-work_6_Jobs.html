By having idols do jobs, they can earn various rewards and increase their fan base. Rewards vary depending on the job location. If the job is a great success, the amount of rewards earned increases. If an idol is in good form, the job will always be a great success.

The amount of reward also increases depending on the idol's intimacy level.

■Job level
The job Lv increases depending on the progress of the task. As the job Lvincreases, the number of fans you can earn upon completing the task also increases.

■Shortening work
You can complete a task by consuming jewels. The amount of jewels consumed increases depending on the remaining time.

■Cancel work
You can cancel a task in progress. If you cancel, you will not receive any rewards.
