During produce, when acquiring skill cards, you can display recommendations and a skill card acquisition guide.
The skill card acquisition guide allows you to compare your owned skill cards with the recommended quantities and also check sample skill card compositions.

■ About Recommendations
When acquiring skill cards, recommended marks appear on skill cards in the sample that you have not yet obtained.

■ About Samples
Displays recommended skill card compositions according to your current PLv.
The sample skill card compositions vary depending on the selected produce idol.
You can change the display settings for the acquisition guide anytime from [Menu > Settings].
