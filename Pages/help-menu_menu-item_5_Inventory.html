In Items, you can check the list of Consumables/Enhancement, Gacha/Exchange, and Pieces.

By opening the item details, you can see the description, quantity owned, expiration date, and main acquisition methods for that item.

■ Consumables/Enhancement
You can check consumable items like "Money" and AP drinks, as well as enhancement items like Support Enhancement Points and Notes.
Some items can be sold and converted into "Money."

■ Gacha/Exchange
You can check items used in "Gacha," such as Platinum Gacha Tickets and Friend Coins, and items used in the "Exchange Shop," like Contest Medals and Flowers.

■ Pieces
You can check the Pieces of Produce Idols.
Pieces can be converted into "Flowers" through Piece Conversion.
