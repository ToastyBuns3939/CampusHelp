There are several types of rankings during the event period.

Event participants are divided into several groups, and rankings are decided within each group.

At the end of the event, you can earn rewards according to your ranking in each ranking.

■Idol High Score Ranking
・This is a ranking of the highest production evaluation in event mode production of the target idols during the event period.
・Rankings are divided by idol.
・The target idols differ for each event.
・Idols who have not completed production in event mode even once during the event period will not participate in the ranking and will not be able to earn rewards.

■Total High Score Ranking
・This is a ranking of the total production evaluation recorded in the idol high score ranking of all target idols.
・There is no total high score ranking for events with only one target idol.
・If you do not complete production in event mode even once during the event period for any of the target idols, they will not participate in the ranking and will not be able to earn rewards.
