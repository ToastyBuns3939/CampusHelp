A "Shop" that updates daily.

The "Daily Exchange Shop" includes the "Money Exchange Shop" and "AP Exchange Shop."
There are limits on the number of exchanges, and they reset with the daily date change.
Exchange counts can also be reset by spending Jewels.

■ Money Exchange Shop
Use "Money" to exchange for various items.

■ AP Exchange Shop
Use AP to exchange for items like "Reward Boost Items" and "Retry Tickets."
"Reward Boost Items" can be set at the start of a Produce session to increase the rewards earned at the end.
You can hold up to 999,999 "Reward Boost Items."
