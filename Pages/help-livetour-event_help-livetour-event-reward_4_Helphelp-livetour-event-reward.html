■ Event Pt Rewards
You can earn Event Pt based on the score obtained from challenging stages.
Rewards can be earned according to the total amount of Event Pt accumulated.

■ Stage Attempt Rewards
Rewards that can be earned by attempting stages.
The amount of rewards varies depending on the score achieved in the stage.

■ Stage Clear Rewards
Rewards earned by clearing a stage. These rewards are granted to all members of the Circle.
When a new round begins, stage clear progress resets, and rewards can be earned again by clearing the stages.
※To claim rewards, you need to go to the Regional Tour Event Top screen.

■ Circle Ranking Rewards
The total Event Pt earned by the Circle is tallied, and the Circle ranking is determined.
After the event tally period ends, rewards will be given to Circle members based on the final ranking.
※The Circle’s total Event Pt is the sum of all members’ individual Event Pt.
※You must have earned at least 1 Event Pt to claim rewards.
