You can equip up to 4 Memories for use in Produce.
Out of the 4, you can rent 1 Memory from a producer you follow by spending *Money*.
You can rent up to 3 times per day.

■ About Skill Cards Obtainable from Rentals
Some Skill Cards may be obtainable even if you haven’t reached their unlock Plv, as long as your Plv is above a certain threshold.
