Achievements related to each idol.
There are common idol achievements, master achievements, and produce idol achievements.

■ Common Idol Achievements
Achievements related to each idol.

■ Master Achievements
Achievements that rank up by accumulating stars in common idol achievements.
Achievements that are the subject of the star count are added separately before and after ranking up.
Details on the total number of stars can be viewed on the master achievement details screen.

■ Produce Idol Achievements
Achievements exclusive to produce idols. When you obtain the target produce idol, it will be added to the achievement, and you can earn the achievement when you meet the conditions.
