■Event Pt Reward
You can challenge a stage and earn Event Pts according to the score you earn.
You can earn rewards according to the total Event Pts you earn.

■Stage Challenge Reward
A reward you can earn by challenging a stage.
The amount of reward you can earn varies depending on the score of the stage you challenge.

■Stage Clear Reward
A reward you can earn by clearing a stage. All members of the circle can earn this reward.
As the round progresses, the stage clear status will be reset, and you can earn rewards by clearing the stage again.

※To receive rewards, you must go to the top of the Regional Tours Event

■Circle Ranking Reward
The points earned by the circle will be tallied and the circle ranking will be determined.
Circle members can earn rewards according to their final ranking after the event tally period ends.

※The points earned by the circle will be the total of the event points earned by the circle members
※You must earn at least 1 event point to receive rewards
