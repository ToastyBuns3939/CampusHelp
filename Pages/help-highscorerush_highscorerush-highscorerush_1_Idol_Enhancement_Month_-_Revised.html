Produce eligible idols in event mode and compete for the highest production rating you can get during the event period.

You can participate by turning on event mode from Produce during the event period, and you can earn rewards based on your position in the high score ranking at the end of the event period and the high score you achieved.

During the event period, you can earn the event item "Starlight" by producing and other activities, and you can use "Starlight" to draw the event gacha.
