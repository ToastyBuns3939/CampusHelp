In the Menu, you can check Produce-related information and settings, as well as pause or retire from Produce.

■ Pause
Saves your current progress and returns to the Home screen.

■ P-Notebook
Check your schedule, growth tasks, and setup.

■ Help
View help related to Produce.

■ Settings
Check and adjust settings during Produce.

■ Achievements
Check the progress and conditions for both the current idol's and shared Produce achievements.

■ Outfit Settings
Set the Produce and Live outfits for your current idol.

■ Retire
Ends the current Produce and returns to Home.
Note: AP used will not be refunded, and you won’t receive a Memory.
Also, it won’t count toward fulfilling the “Let’s Produce” mission condition.
