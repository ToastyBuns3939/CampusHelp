Performance battles will be held at each stage, and the producer who wins at least two of the three stages will be declared the winner of that battle.

■Winning or losing each stage
In each stage, the idols in the group will compete in performances. The producer with the highest total score will be declared the winner of that stage.
The idol who gets the highest score in the stage will receive a bonus on the score they get, which will be added to their total score.
The contest battles will basically follow the same rules as the exams you are producing, but all actions are performed automatically.

※For details, see "Exam flow" in the help section "Lessons/Exams"
