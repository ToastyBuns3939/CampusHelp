In Job Activities, you primarily gain fan votes and raise your parameters.
Different business types and dialogue choices during commus can trigger various bonus effects.
Some business types may require stamina or P-Points to perform.

■Types of Jobs
There are four types of Job activities:
・Corporate Events
Gain Skill Cards and P-Drinks
・Municipal Events
Gain Skill Cards and P-Points
・Resort Facilities
Gain Skill Cards and recover stamina
・Commercial Facilities
Gain enhanced Skill Cards

■Job Success
Job activities can occasionally result in a Great Success, granting more fan votes than usual.
The higher your current parameters, the higher the chance of a Great Success.
