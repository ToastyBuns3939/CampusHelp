You can watch various commus such as Hatsuboshi Commus and Idol Commus.

■ Commu Playback Settings
Before playing a commu, you can change screen and voice settings.

■ Playing Commus
Tap the screen or "AUTO" to progress the commu. With "AUTO", the commu advances automatically.

■ Functions During Commu Playback
・AUTO
The commu progresses automatically without tapping. You can also set it to 1.5x speed.
・Fast Forward
Speeds up the commu playback.
・Skip
Skips the commu entirely.
・Screen Rotation
Allows you to rotate your device's screen for viewing.
・Log
You can check the idols’ dialogue and other lines from the commu.
・Hide UI
Hides the on-screen UI.
