■ Live Photography
You can watch each idol’s unlocked lives. During the live, you can take photos, save them to the photo album, and set them on your business card.
You can also save them to your device. The photo album can store up to 100 photos.

■ Idol Photography
You can take photos of idols at locations. Photos can be saved to the photo album and set on your business card.
You can also save them to your device. The photo album can store up to 100 photos.
