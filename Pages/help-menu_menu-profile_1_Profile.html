You can check and set your profile.

■ Profile Info Check/Settings
You can check your P-ID, birthday, and producer name, and also edit them.
You can also set your business card.

■ Basic Info
You can view your basic information.
You can set your rental Support Card and Memory. By setting them, other Producers can rent them at the start of Produce, helping with training.
You’ll receive "Friend Coins" based on the number of times your cards are rented. Earned "Friend Coins" are sent to "Presents."
※Friend Coins can be claimed from "Presents" within 30 days of being rented.

■ Activity History
You can check your overall game progress and highest Produce evaluations.

■ Report Card
You can review each idol’s progress, achievement records, and your Produce history.
Report card content is updated regularly.
