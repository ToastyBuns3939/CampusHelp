These are idols that can be produced.

■Rarity
There are three types of rarity: R/SR/SSR.

■Plan
This indicates the direction of production, and is determined for each produced idol. The types of skill cards, P-drinks, and P-items that appear vary depending on the plan.

■Initial parameters
The initial values ​​of the parameters developed in production.

■Parameter bonus
If an idol has a parameter bonus, a bonus is applied to the parameters that increase at the end of the lesson. The larger the parameter bonus, the larger the value of this bonus.

■Recommended effect
The recommended effect for each produced idol.

■Skill card
A skill card unique to the produced idol that can be used in production.

■P-item
A P-item unique to the produced idol that has an effect activated in production.

■Stamina
The maximum stamina during production.

■Produce idol commu
Produced idols may have commus related to the produce idol.
You can see them in production or in "Commu".

■ Songs
When you acquire a produced idol who has a song, you will be able to listen to that song in "Music."

The live performance in production may also change.

■ Costumes
When you acquire a produced idol who has a costume, you will be able to set it in "Clothes."

■ Hairstyles
When you acquire a produced idol who has a hairstyle, you will be able to set it in "Clothes."
