Link Missions are missions that circle members can cooperate to challenge. To participate in Link Missions, you must belong to a circle.

Progress in Link Missions is shared among circle members, and when completed, all members receive mission clear rewards.

The content of Link Missions differs for each circle.

Mission progress advances by completing a produce session while meeting all mission conditions.

Mission conditions are hidden at the start and hints unlocking in order from one as the mission progresses.

Hints provide the conditions required to clear the target mission.

The MVP is the circle member who contributed the most to the mission progress.
