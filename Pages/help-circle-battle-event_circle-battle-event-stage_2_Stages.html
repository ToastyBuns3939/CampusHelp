In the Regional Tours event, multiple stages with different judging criteria and stage information will appear.
In each stage, the organized idols will perform. You can earn scores by performing, and you can earn event points according to the scores you earn.
The performance progresses according to the same rules as the exam during production, but all actions are automatic.

※For details, please refer to the "Exam Flow" and "Contest" in the help section "Lessons/Exams".

■About clearing a stage
Each stage has a required score to clear, and the score acquisition status according to the stage is shared within the circle. By reaching the required score to clear the stage, you will clear it and earn stage clear rewards.
When all stages are cleared, the score acquisition status of each stage is reset and the round progresses. The clear score and stage information of each stage will change depending on the round.

※If you earn a score higher than the required score to clear a stage during a stage challenge, you will earn event points including the excess score.

■About the number of attempts you can make
There is a set number of attempts you can make per day, and when the limit of the number of attempts you can make is reached, you will no longer be able to make attempts that day. The number of attempts you can make will be restored according to the date update.
