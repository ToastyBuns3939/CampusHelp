You can use Exchange Points to acquire the target Produce Idols, support cards, etc.
Exchange Points can only be acquired/used if Exchange Points are set for the "Gacha".

■How to acquire Exchange Points
You can acquire 1 Exchange Point each time you acquire a Produce Idol or support card by drawing a "Gacha".

※Regardless of the rarity of the acquired Produce Idol/support card, you can acquire 1 Exchange Point.
※Even if you acquire a Produce Idol/support card that you already own, you can only acquire 1 Exchange Point.
※If you use a "Gacha" ticket to draw a "Gacha", you can acquire Exchange Points if Exchange Points are set for the "Gacha".

■How to use Exchange Points
You can acquire Produce Idols, support cards, etc. in the "Exchange List" by using Exchange Points during the period of the "Gacha" that is being held. If you select a Produce Idol that you have already acquired, you can acquire pieces of the Produce Idol according to the target.
*You can keep the acquired exchange points and exchange them for the applicable "gacha" until the deadline of each "gacha".

■Flowers
When the "gacha" period ends, the exchange points will be converted into "Flowers".
"Flowers" can be exchanged for items at the "Exchange".
