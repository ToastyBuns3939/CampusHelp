If you are not a member of a circle, you can join one. There are three ways to apply to join.

■Circle List
You can send an application to join the circle you want to join from the automatically displayed list of recommended circles.

■Condition Search
You can search by specifying the conditions for the circle you want to join and send an application to the circle you want to join.

■ID Search
You can send an application to a circle by specifying the circle ID.

If the circle you sent your application to approves your application automatically, you can join immediately. If it approves your application, you can join after the leader of that circle approves it.

※You will not be able to join another circle for a certain period of time after establishing, leaving, or disbanding a circle.
