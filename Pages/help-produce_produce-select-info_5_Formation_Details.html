In Formation Details, you can check various details when producing with the current formation.

■ Cards / Items
You can check the skill cards you have at the start of production and the skill cards and P-items you can obtain during production. Skill cards and P items are determined by the chosen produce idol’s plan, owned skill cards, equipped support cards, and equipped memories.

■ Abilities
You can check lesson supports, support abilities, and memory abilities that activate during production. Lesson supports are determined by the equipped support cards. Support abilities are also determined by the equipped support cards, while memory abilities depend on the equipped memories.

■ Events
You can check support events that may occur during production. Support events are determined by the equipped support cards. When a support event occurs, various effects such as obtaining skill cards or P-items will activate.
