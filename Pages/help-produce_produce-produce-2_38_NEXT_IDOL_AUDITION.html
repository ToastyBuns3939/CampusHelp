This is a scenario where you participate in the "NEXT IDOL AUDITION" and aim for the championship live.
It unlocks by clearing P-Task 35 and reaching bond level 10 with any idol.
Only idols with bond level 10 or higher can challenge it.

■ Schedule features
・By passing the first, second, and final auditions, the idol can appear in the live show
・You can choose "Rest" up to 4 times during the produce

※If there are no selectable schedules and no remaining "Rest" choices, that week will be skipped automatically
