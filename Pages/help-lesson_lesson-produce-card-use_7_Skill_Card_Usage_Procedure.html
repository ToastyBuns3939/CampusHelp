When using skill cards, stamina is basically consumed, but if you have energy, energy is consumed first.
Some skill cards consume stamina directly, ignoring energy.
If a certain time passes while selecting a skill card, recommended cards suited to the situation will be suggested.

■ Deck
All skill cards you have at the start of the lesson are placed here.
At the start of each turn, the top 3 cards of the deck are moved to your hand.
If the deck runs out of cards, the discard pile is shuffled to form a new deck.

■ Hand
Skill cards that can be used during the turn.
At the start of the turn, you draw 3 cards from the top of the deck into your hand.
Only one card can be used per turn.
The number of cards drawn or usable may change due to skill cards or P items.
You can hold up to 5 cards in your hand at once.
If adding a skill card to your hand would exceed this limit, the card is placed on top of the deck instead.

■ Discard Pile
Used skill cards are placed here.
Cards used only once per lesson are excluded from this pile for the duration of that lesson.
At the end of the turn, any unused cards in your hand are moved to the discard pile.
