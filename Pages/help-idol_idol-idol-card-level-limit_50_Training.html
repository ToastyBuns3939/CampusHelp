You can use "Notes" and "Money" to train your produce idols. Training will increase their initial parameters and maximum stamina.
In addition, when they reach a certain training stage, their illustration will change and their unique skill card will be strengthened.
As the training stage increases further, P-Idol abilities will be unlocked or strengthened.
Training items can be obtained through "Produce", "Work", and "Circle" requests.
