These are cards that can be arranged in Produce. Arranging them activates various effects that support the Produce. You can arrange 6 cards during Produce, with 1 card being a support card rented from another producer.

■ Rarity
There are 3 rarities: R, SR, and SSR.

■ Level
Raising the level strengthens the effects of support abilities. It can also unlock new support events and support abilities. See each section for details.

■ Type
There are 4 types: Vocal, Dance, Visual, and Assist.
Vocal, Dance, and Visual types have effects that make it easier to train their respective parameters.
Assist type has effects that assist clearing Produce, such as stamina recovery and acquiring P-points.

■ Arrangement Conditions
Some support cards have restrictions on which plans they can be arranged in.
