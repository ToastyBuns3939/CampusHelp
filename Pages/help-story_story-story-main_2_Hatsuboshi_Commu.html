These commus are unlocked by completing Hatsuboshi Tasks. During playback of a Hatsuboshi Commu, the following features are available:

■ Subtitles
You can toggle between “Show All Subtitles” and “Show Partial Subtitles.”

■ Dialogue Skip
Tap the left or right edge of the screen to skip to the previous or next line of dialogue.

■ Playback Speed
You can change the playback speed of the commu.

■ Screen Orientation
You can switch between landscape and portrait playback. In portrait mode, the commu log will be displayed.
