The plan indicates the direction of production and is set individually for each produce idol. The skill cards and types of P-Drinks that appear vary depending on the plan.

■Sense
A plan that focuses on growth by utilizing "Good Shape" and "Focus."

・Good Shape
Increases parameter gains by 50%.

・Focus
For each 1 Focus gained, parameter gains increase by 1.

・Excellent Shape
Increases the parameter gain bonus of Good Shape by 10% for each turn it is active.
