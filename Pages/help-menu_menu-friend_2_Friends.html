You can check your follows and followers.
Support cards and memories from friends you follow can be rented at the start of Produce.
■ Follow
You can follow other producers by the following methods:

・Enter the producer’s P-ID via ID search to follow.
・Follow the producer of a support card rented at the start of Produce after the Produce ends.
・Follow recommended producers from the Recommended tab within Friends.

※You can follow up to 30 people.
※If the other person’s follower count is full, you cannot follow them.

■ Followers
You can check producers who follow you. Producers who mutually follow you are marked as “Mutual Follow.”
