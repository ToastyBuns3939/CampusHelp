You can create a business card. You can set images such as idols, support cards, and photos, and arrange business card parts for editing.

Business cards are displayed to other producers in contests and other events.

You can also save business cards from other producers.

■ About Custom Business Cards
Custom business cards allow more freedom in editing business cards.

・You can set videos on business cards. You can cut out and set scenes you want from your favorite live performances.
・You can place up to 100 backgrounds and parts, each editable with rotation, scaling, and cropping.
・More backgrounds and parts become available, including community backgrounds and achievements.

■ About SNS Sharing
You can display your business card with a business card code, which other producers can scan.
Also, when posting on SNS, you can generate a link, allowing other producers to access your profile within Gakuen Idolmaster via the link.

※SNS sharing is not available in the DMM GAMES version.
