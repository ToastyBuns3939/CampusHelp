You can earn achievements by achieving various conditions.
■Acquisition/Ranking up of achievements
Each achievement has achievement conditions, and you can see it from the details of each achievement. By achieving the conditions, you can earn achievements. In addition, there are some items that have further conditions for earned achievements. In that case, achieving the conditions will increase the rank of achievement.
■Achievement Rewards
Earnings/upgrading achievements can earn rewards such as "Producer Exp" and Jewel.
You can earn an achievement by moving to each achievement screen after meeting the acquisition criteria.
