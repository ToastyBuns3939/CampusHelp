The plan indicates the direction of production and is set individually for each produce idol. The skill cards and types of P-Drinks that appear vary depending on the plan.

■Anomaly
A plan that focuses on growth by utilizing the guidelines "Full Power," "Confident," and "Preservation."

・Full Power
If you start a turn with 10 or more Full Power Points, 10 are consumed and you enter the "Full Power" state.
In the "Full Power" state, parameter gains increase by 200%, and you gain 1 extra skill card use.
"Full Power" ends at the end of the turn.
When the guideline is set to "Full Power," it cannot be changed to another.

・Confident
Parameter gains and stamina consumption increase.
"Confident" has 2 stages. Activating "Confident" again while already in stage 1 will increase it to stage 2.
The effects strengthen depending on the stage.
Stage 1 increases parameter gains and stamina consumption by 100%.
Stage 2 increases parameter gains by 150% and stamina consumption by 100%. Also, using a skill card directly consumes 1 stamina per use.

・Preservation
Parameter gains and stamina consumption decrease.
When you change your guideline from the "Preservation" state, parameter gains increase for 1 turn and you gain 1 extra skill card use.
"Preservation" has 2 stages. Activating "Preservation" again while already in stage 1 will increase it to stage 2.
The effects strengthen depending on the stage.
Stage 1 reduces parameter gains and stamina consumption by 50%. When it's lifted, Enthusiasm increases by 5, and you gain 1 extra skill card use.
Stage 2 reduces parameter gains and stamina consumption by 75%. When it's lifted, Enthusiasm increases by 8 and Energy increases by 5. You also gain 1 extra skill card use.

・Enthusiasm
For each 1 Enthusiasm gained, parameter gains increase by 1.
Enthusiasm resets to 0 at the end of the turn.

・Growth
Growth enhances the effects written on skill cards. It is granted when certain conditions are met or when certain skill card effects activate.

・On Hold
"On Hold" lets you keep skill cards in the deck that you want to draw when entering "Full Power."
When "Full Power" is triggered, the held skill cards are added to your hand.
You can hold up to 2 skill cards. If more than 2 are held, the extra ones are sent to the discard pile in the order they were held.
