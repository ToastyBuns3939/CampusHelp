■ What are Another Idols?
Another Idols are different versions of Produce Idols that have appeared before (Original Produce Idols).
They share the same status, training stages/talent blooming stages, skill cards, P-items, etc. as the original Produce Idols, but they have exclusive songs and costumes.
You can produce them even if you only have Another Idol.
If you have both the original Produce Idol and Another Idol, you can switch between them by producing. Go to [Home > Produce] or [Home > You can switch between them in [P Idol Development].

■Elements exclusive to Another Idol
The exclusive elements that Another Idol has are as follows.
・Image
・Song
・Costume

■About costumes
・There are no different colored costumes for Another Idol.
・The different colored costumes that can be obtained at the 4th stage of talent blooming for the original version of a Produce Idol can be obtained by owning the original version of the Produce Idol.
・You cannot obtain them by reaching the 4th stage of talent blooming when you only own the Another Idol

■About Produce Idol Pieces
・Pieces are the same for both the original version of a Produce Idol and Another Idol.
・If you acquire Another Idol while you own the original version of a Produce Idol, you can obtain pieces for the original version of the Produce Idol.
・If you acquire the original version of a Produce Idol while you own the Another Idol, you can obtain pieces for the original version of the Produce Idol.
・The original version of a Produce Idol can only be obtained by owning the Another Idol. You can unlock them with pieces even if you already have them.
・Another Idol cannot be unlocked with pieces.
・You can blossom if you only have Another Idol.

■About achievements
・Achievements are the same for the original version of Produce Idols and Another Idols.
・You can progress in achievements as long as you have either one.

■About business card backgrounds
・There are no business card backgrounds for Another Idols.
・If you obtain Another Idol without having the original version of Produce Idol, you will obtain the business card background of the original version of Produce Idol.

■About Produce Idol Commu
・There are no Produce Idol Commu for Another Idols.
・If you obtain Another Idol even if you do not have the original version of Produce Idol, the Produce Idol Communities for the original version of Produce Idol will be unlocked.
※The Produce Idol Communities for the original version of Produce Idol will not occur while producing Another Idol.
