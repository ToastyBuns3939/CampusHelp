You can use coins earned in-game to draw from the "Gacha."

■ Friend Gacha
This "Gacha" can be drawn using "Friend Coins" earned by having your support cards or memories rented out. You can obtain items like training materials.

■ Sense Gacha / Logic Gacha / Anomaly Gacha
These "Gacha" use "Sense Coins," "Logic Coins," or "Anomaly Coins." You can obtain support cards, training materials, and other items.
"Sense Coins," "Logic Coins," and "Anomaly Coins" can be earned through "Produce" and other activities.
